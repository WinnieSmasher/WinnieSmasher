### About me

I am a graduate student at the Space Science Institute, Macau University of Science and Technology.

My current interests are around space science data, lunar and planetary applications, ground penetrating radar simulation, computer vision, and Python tools for research workflows.

I am still early in the process, so I try to keep this profile honest: less about polished achievements, more about what I am learning, building, testing, and slowly improving.

### What I am exploring

- Space science data analysis and lunar / planetary computer vision
- GPR and FDTD simulation workflows, especially around gprMax
- Python research tooling, data cleaning, and reproducible experiments
- Low-frequency A-share quant research with careful data quality checks
- Practical AI-assisted coding for research, automation, and code reading
